Research Partnerships with the University of Windsor

The University of Windsor highly values and actively supports research and collaboration with industry, non-profit, government organizations, and community partners. Our world-class researchers collaborate to bring new ideas and technologies to the local, national, and global markets. The Research Partnerships team also manages the University of Windsor's Intellectual Property (IP), and service contract portfolio by drafting mutually beneficial and sustainable partnership agreements.

Partnering with us has the following advantages:

  • Access to our elite specialists
  • Access to our graduate and undergraduate talent pool
  • Access to our cutting-edge research centers and institutes
  • Government program access to maximize your R&D investments

To connect, please reach out to the Research Partnerships team
