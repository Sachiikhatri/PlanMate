This includes respect for dignity, independence, integration and equal opportunity as recognized in the University's policies and the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 (AODA). If you are a student and you would like more information about how the University of Windsor can accommodate your needs on campus, please visit Student Accessibility Services.
