Continuing Education courses may be eligible for OSAP Application for Micro-credentials. Details are provided in the individual course program pages. Continuing Education courses are not eligible for full or part-time financial aid funding (i.e. OSAP), nor scholarships, bursaries, and awards offered to traditional credit-bearing programs. Inquiries regarding financial aid for academic students should be directed to the Office of Student Award and Financial Aid.
