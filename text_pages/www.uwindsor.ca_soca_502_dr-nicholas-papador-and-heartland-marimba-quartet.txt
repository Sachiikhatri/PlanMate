19:30 Dr. Nicholas Papador and the Heartland Marimba Quartet Free Lecture-Recital Tuesday, November 12, 2024, 7:30pm Armouries Performance Hall, Room 132 353 Freedom Way Dr. Nicholas Papador and the world-class Heartland Marimba Quartet (HMQ) will perform and discuss selections from Papador's collection of klezmer suites for mallet percussion entitled Vessels of Song. The presentation will provide a precedent for klezmer performance on mallet instruments through contributions of a small but significant line of performers. Attendees will hear and learn about enjoyable new ensemble literature with poignant historical and cultural significance. The program includes music from Papador's new arrangements: The Forebearers Suite, The Philadelphia Suite, The New York Suites (#1 & #2), and the Ukrainian Suite.
