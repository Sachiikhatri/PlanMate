Guided by our strategic plan (Aspire: Together for Tomorrow), the office oversees long-range academic planning, teaching and learning, faculty innovation, student life and academic success, enrolment management, and institutional analysis. We are committed to promoting equity, sustainability, and the rich diversity of our campus.
